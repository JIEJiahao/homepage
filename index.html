JIE Jiahao(Howie)

Howie, borned in Wuhan, PR China, is a master student of statistics at the University of Hong Kong. During the period of undergraduate, Howie majored in insurance(actuarial science oriented) and obtained the bachelors degree of economics. From May to August in 2021, he was employed as a junior business analyst at EIS Co. Ltd. which focuses on IT development of insurance core system. He was responsible for using OpenL, a logistics program language, to build premium rating module for 2 insurance companies. Now, Howie is interested in exploring the application of statistics methods in financial region. Let’s wish him good luck! :P
